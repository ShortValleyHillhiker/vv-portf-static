Tools I Keep Coming Back To

A short and partial list

January 5, 2025

Figma for most things, but not because it’s the best tool for any one thing. It’s the best tool for working with other people, which turns out to matter more.

iA Writer when I need to think through something in prose. The lack of options is the feature. I’ve tried to replace it several times and always come back.

A physical notebook. Unlined, medium size, whatever pen is nearby. I don’t have a system. The point is that it’s slow — slow enough that only the things worth writing get written.

Terminal + a text editor for anything code-adjacent. I’ve gone through phases with different editors. Right now it’s whatever is open.

The pattern I notice: the tools I keep are the ones that don’t try to do everything. The ones that know what they are.
